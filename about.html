About

Guitarist Joe Wilga and songwriter Jed Pavlovich began their musical collaboration 5 years ago, meeting at an "Open Mic-less" jam session in Harlem.

They spent the next 3 years honing their studio skills on countless tracks for underground NYC hip-hop heavyweights before forming Coilquo out of a need to beat the winter blues. Wilga's smooth riffs and interstellar soundscapes set the stage for Pavlovich's raw lyricism and frantic synths. Precision grooves by drummer James Jones (Sunlord, Terminal Fury) and bassist Mx## round out the Coilquo sound, a combination of dirty blues, classic r&b, 70s and 90s rock, fusion and soul.
